Refund Policy

Last Updated: August 15, 2024 At Annex Financial, we strive to ensure that our clients are satisfied with our services. However, we understand that there may be instances where you may wish to request a refund. This Refund Policy outlines the terms under which refunds may be provided. 1. Eligibility for Refunds Refunds may be issued under the following conditions: Service Cancellation: If you cancel a service before it has been fully rendered, you may be eligible for a partial or full refund, depending on the progress of the service and the terms outlined in your service agreement. Unsatisfactory Service: If you are not satisfied with the service provided, you must notify us in writing within 30 days of the service completion for a potential refund or service correction. 2. Non-Refundable Services Certain fees and services are non-refundable, including but not limited to: Initial consultation fees; Services that have been fully rendered; Fees for financial products purchased through third parties; Any processing or administrative fees outlined in your agreement. 3. Refund Request Process To request a refund, please follow these steps: Contact Us: Email us at refunds@annexfin.com or call us at +1 (877) 309-1602 within 30 days of your service to initiate the refund process. Provide Details: Include your name, account information, details of the service in question, and the reason for your refund request. Review: Your request will be reviewed by our team within 10 business days. We may request additional information if needed. 4. Refund Method If a refund is approved, it will be issued via the original payment method used at the time of purchase. Refunds typically take 7-14 business days to process. 5. Changes to Refund Policy Annex Financial reserves the right to modify this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page and will apply to services purchased after the policy has been updated. 6. Contact Information For any questions or concerns regarding this Refund Policy, please contact us at: Annex Financial Group, LLC 429 Fourth Ave, Suite 300 Pittsburgh, PA 15219 Phone: +1 (877) 309-1602 Email: support@annexfin.com
